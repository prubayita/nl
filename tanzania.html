Tanzania

Safari Heart of Africa

Land of Natural Wonders

From the endless Serengeti plains to the spice islands of Zanzibar, Tanzania offers Africa's most iconic safari experiences.

Serengeti National Park

Witness the Great Migration of 2.5 million wildebeest across endless golden plains.

Ngorongoro Crater

World's largest intact volcanic caldera teeming with Big Five wildlife.

Zanzibar Archipelago

Pristine beaches, spice tours, and historic Stone Town's winding alleys.

Best Safari Seasons

Optimal periods for wildlife viewing and beach holidays

June – October

Dry Season | Peak Migration

  • Best for Serengeti river crossings
  • Ideal climbing conditions for Kilimanjaro
  • Superb beach weather in Zanzibar

November – December

Short Rains | Green Season

  • Lush landscapes with newborn animals
  • Fewer crowds in northern parks
  • Excellent birdwatching opportunities

January – February

Calving Season | Whale Sharks

  • Wildebeest calving in southern Serengeti
  • Whale shark sightings off Mafia Island
  • Prime time for photography
