Volcanoes National Park

4 Days in Rwanda

Explore Rwanda's culture, history, and wildlife in this unforgettable 4-day experience, including gorilla trekking, golden monkeys, and Kigali's vibrant city life.

Day 1: Pick Up and Transfer to the Hotel in Kigali

Welcome to Rwanda! Upon arrival at Kigali International Airport, you'll be greeted by your guide with a special welcome package. Enjoy a scenic drive through Rwanda's hilly landscape before arriving at your hotel. In the afternoon, visit the Kigali Genocide Memorial, explore a local market, enjoy lunch in Kigali, and continue with a 3-hour drive to Volcanoes National Park.

Accommodation: Kigali Serena Hotel

Day 2: Kigali City Tour to Volcanoes National Park

Depart Kigali for Musanze and check in to your hotel. Visit the Dian Fossey Exhibition to learn about gorilla conservation and the Virunga Massif. Return to your hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Accommodation: Sambola Lodge

Day 3: Gorilla Trekking

After an early breakfast, head to the park headquarters for a briefing and group assignment. Begin the once-in-a-lifetime gorilla trekking experience. Return to the lodge in the afternoon for relaxation.

Accommodation: Sambola Lodge

Day 4: Golden Monkey Trekking and Departure

After breakfast, meet your guide and head for golden monkey trekking. Watch these playful, elusive primates in their natural habitat. Afterward, return to the lodge for lunch and drive back to Kigali for your international departure.

What to bring: Rain jacket, hiking shoes, flexible trousers.
