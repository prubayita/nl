Uganda

Pearl of Africa

Where Wilderness Meets Wonder

Discover Uganda's unique blend of primate encounters and classic safaris. From mountain gorilla trekking in misty forests to thrilling chimpanzee tracking and Nile adventures, experience Africa's most biodiverse destination.

Bwindi Impenetrable NP

Home to half the world's mountain gorillas. Trek through ancient rainforests for life-changing encounters.

Kibale National Park

Primate capital of Africa with 13 species including chimpanzees. Guided forest walks and habituation experiences.

Queen Elizabeth NP

Diverse ecosystems with tree-climbing lions, boat safaris on Kazinga Channel, and volcanic crater lakes.

Best Time for Uganda Safaris

Optimal periods for primate tracking and wildlife viewing

June – September

Dry Season | Peak Tracking

  • Best conditions for gorilla trekking
  • Ideal for chimpanzee habituation
  • Excellent photographic conditions

December – February

Short Dry Season | Birding

  • Second dry season for wildlife viewing
  • Prime time for migratory birds
  • Lower trekking permit demand

March – May

Green Season | Value

  • Lush landscapes and newborn animals
  • Best rates for luxury lodges
  • Fewer visitors in parks
