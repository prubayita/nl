Kenya

Land of the Great Migration

Experience the ultimate African safari in Kenya, where iconic wildlife and breathtaking landscapes converge. From the Great Migration in Masai Mara to the snow-capped peaks of Mount Kenya, discover a world of adventure.

Masai Mara

Witness the Great Migration and encounter the Big Five in Africa's most famous wildlife reserve.

Amboseli

Photograph elephants against the backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro in this iconic national park.

Tsavo National Parks

Explore Kenya's largest protected area, home to legendary "red elephant" and diverse ecosystems.

Best Time for Kenyan Safaris

Optimal periods for wildlife viewing and beach vacations

July – October

Peak Migration Season | Dry Weather

  • Witness the Great Migration river crossings
  • Optimal game viewing in dry conditions
  • Best beach weather on the coast

November – March

Green Season | Birdwatching

  • Lush landscapes and newborn animals
  • Excellent bird migration viewing
  • Lower rates and fewer crowds

April – June

Long Rains | Value Season

  • Best rates for luxury lodges
  • Spectacular scenery with blooming flora
  • Ideal for photography enthusiasts
